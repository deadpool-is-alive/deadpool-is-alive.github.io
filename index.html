01 — About

I build experiences at the intersection of code, data and creativity.

I'm a Civil Enginnering undergraduate at IIT Kanpur with a deep fascination for how things work — from data to model, from model to prediction, from dev to deploy. My journey to start learning coding started with me first mind-blown that how it was simple to get started with web development and then this grew also to making my own games and Currently, I am now hooked on learning to create with data and interactive software ever since.

These days I split my time between three worlds: studying algorithms and systems from internet, shipping production software as an engineering intern, and crafing models on weekends. Each feeds the other in unexpected ways.

I care about feel — that hard-to-define quality that makes software satisfying to use. Whether it's a 90% accuracy of the model or a butter-smooth page transition on the web. the details matter.
